<p><span class="foreign"><strong>Manifest destiny</strong></span>, &quot;that which clearly appears destined to come to pass; a future state, condition, or event which can be foreseen with certainty, or is regarded as inevitable&quot; was much used in American politics from about the time of the Mexican War &quot;by those who believed that the United States were destined in time to occupy the entire continent&quot; [Century Dictionary].</p>
<blockquote>Other nations have tried to check ... the fulfillment of our manifest destiny to overspread the Continent allotted by Providence for the free development of our yearly multiplying millions.
