Internal evidence suggests the West Germanic word is pre-4c. and originally meant the fencing, not the place enclosed. Some later senses in English represent later borrowings from French. OED discounts notion of a Celtic origin. Welsh <span class="foreign">parc</span>, Gaelic <span class="foreign">pairc</span> are from English.
<br/><br/>

Applied to sporting fields in American English from 1867.
