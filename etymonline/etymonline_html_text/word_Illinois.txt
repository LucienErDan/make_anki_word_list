<p>The place appears in a 1673 text as <span class="foreign">Moinguena</span>, and historians believe this represents Miami-Illinois <span class="foreign">mooyiinkweena</span>, literally &quot;shitface,&quot; from <span class="foreign">mooy</span> &quot;excrement&quot; + <span class="foreign">iinkwee</span> &quot;face;&quot; a name given by the <span class="crossreference">Peoria</span> tribe (whose name has itself become a sort of insult) to their western neighbors.
