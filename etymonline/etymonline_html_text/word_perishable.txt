<p>A living element in English, used in new formations from either Latin or native words (<span class="foreign">readable</span>, <span class="foreign">bearable</span>) and also with nouns (<span class="foreign">objectionable</span>, <span class="foreign">peaceable</span>). Sometimes with an active signification (<span class="foreign">suitable</span>, <span class="foreign">capable</span>), sometimes of neutral signification (<span class="foreign">durable</span>, <span class="foreign">conformable</span>). It has become very elastic in meaning, as in a <span class="foreign">reliable witness</span>, a <span class="foreign">playable foul ball</span>, <span class="foreign">perishable goods</span>.
