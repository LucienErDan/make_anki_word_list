<p>&quot;Hence: &quot;a counter-register as a token of ownership used to check against, and prevent, loss or theft&quot; (as in <span class="foreign">hat check</span>, etc.), 1812.  Hence also the financial use for &quot;written order for money drawn on a bank, money draft&quot; (1798, often spelled <span class="foreign">cheque</span>), which was probably influenced by <span class="crossreference">exchequer</span>.
