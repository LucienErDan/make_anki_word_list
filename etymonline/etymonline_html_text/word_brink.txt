<p>Associated with the policies advocated by John Foster Dulles, U.S. Secretary of State 1953-1959. The word springs from Dulles&#x27; description of his philosophy in a magazine interview [with Time-Life Washington bureau chief James Shepley] in early 1956:</p>
<blockquote>The ability to get to the verge without getting into the war is the necessary art. If you cannot master it, you inevitably get into war. If you try to run away from it, if you are scared to go to the brink, you are lost.</blockquote>
