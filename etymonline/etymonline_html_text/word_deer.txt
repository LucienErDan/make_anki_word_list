<p>This is perhaps from PIE <span class="foreign">*dheusom</span> &quot;creature that breathes,&quot; from root <span class="foreign">*dheu-</span> (1) &quot;cloud, breath&quot; (source also of Lithuanian <span class="foreign">dusti</span> &quot;gasp,&quot; <span class="foreign">dvėsti</span> &quot;gasp, perish;&quot; Old Church Slavonic <span class="foreign">dychati</span> &quot;breathe&quot;). The more restricted sense of the word </p>
