From late 14c. in alchemy as &quot;volatile substance; distillate;&quot; from c. 1500 as &quot;substance capable of uniting the fixed and the volatile elements of the philosopher&#x27;s stone.&quot; Hence <span class="foreign">spirits</span> &quot;volatile substance;&quot; sense narrowed to &quot;strong alcoholic liquor&quot; by 1670s. <br/><br/>
