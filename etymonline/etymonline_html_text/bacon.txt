<blockquote>&quot;Now there is a proposition that we issue $500,000,000 or $1,000,000,000 of bonds for a waterway, and then that we just apportion part to the Mississippi and part to the Atlantic, a part to the Missouri and a part to the Ohio. <p>The magazine article that includes the quote opens with:</p>
<blockquote>We doubt whether any one knows how or when, or from what application of what story, the phrase &quot;the National pork barrel&quot; has come into use.
