<p>The Portuguese adjective is from Latin <span class="foreign">facticius</span> &quot;made by art, artificial,&quot; from <span class="foreign">facere</span> &quot;to make, do, produce&quot; (from PIE root <span class="crossreference">*dhe-</span> &quot;to set, put;&quot; compare French <span class="foreign">factice</span> &quot;artificial,&quot; restored from Old French <span class="foreign">faitise</span>, from Latin <span class="foreign">facticius</span>).
