<p><span class="foreign"><strong>Flying fish</strong></span> is from 1510s; <span class="foreign"><strong>flying buttress</strong></span> &quot;segment of an arch projecting from a solid mass and serving to stabilize a wall&quot; is from 1660s. <span class="foreign"><strong>Flying Dutchman</strong></span>, ghost ship off the Cape of Good Hope, is attested since 1790 [John MacDonald &quot;Travels in Various Parts of Europe, Asia, and Africa&quot;]. <p>Meaning &quot;one who has lost the power of resistance to some habit or vice&quot; is from 1550s.
