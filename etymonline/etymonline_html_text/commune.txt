<blockquote>I am not aware that any English word precisely corresponds to the general term of the original. In France every association of human dwellings forms a <span class="foreign">commune</span>, and every commune is governed by a <span class="foreign">Maire</span> and a <span class="foreign">Conseil municipal</span>. <p>The English word sometimes was used in reference to the idealistic communities formed in U.S. c. 1840s, inspired by Fourier and Owen, and was used in late 1960s of hippie settlements established along similar lines.</p>
