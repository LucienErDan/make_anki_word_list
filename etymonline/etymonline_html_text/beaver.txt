It has been assumed that the hero&#x27;s name was given to the animal because he was a noted healer and the odorous reddish-brown secretions of the inguinal sacs of the animal (Latin <span class="foreign">castoreum</span>), were used medicinally in ancient times, especially for women&#x27;s diseases. The Greek word replaced the native Latin word for &quot;beaver&quot; (<span class="foreign">fiber</span>).</p>
