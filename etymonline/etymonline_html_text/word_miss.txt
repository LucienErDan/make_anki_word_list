<p>Sense of &quot;fail to find&quot; (someone or something) is by late 12c. Meaning &quot;fail to note, perceive, or observe&quot; is from early 13c. Meaning &quot;omit, leave out, skip&quot; is by mid-14c. Sense of &quot;to escape, avoid&quot; is from 1520s.</p>

His eye, keen and penetrating, discovered at a glance that I had just left him room to pass.
