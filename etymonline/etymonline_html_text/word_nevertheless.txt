<p>Weekley calls it &quot;A very puzzling word.&quot; Sense of &quot;a rush of water&quot; in a stream (1520s), is probably from <span class="crossreference">flush</span> (v.1). Independently from the verb, probably, is the noun sense of &quot;a flight of birds suddenly started up&quot; (1590s).</p>
