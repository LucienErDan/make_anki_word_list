Spanish <span class="foreign">alcazar</span> &quot;castle&quot; is from Arabic <span class="foreign">al-qasr</span>, from Latin <span class="foreign">castrum</span>. <span class="foreign"><strong>Castles in Spain</strong></span> &quot;visionary project, vague imagination of possible wealth&quot; translates 14c. The statement that an (English) man&#x27;s home is his castle is from 16c.</p>
