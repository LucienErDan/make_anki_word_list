<p>As a method of execution, in late Old English (but originally specifically of crucifixion). <p>Teen slang sense of &quot;spend time&quot; first recorded 1951; <span class="foreign"><strong>hang around</strong></span> &quot;idle, loiter&quot; is from 1828, American English; also compare <span class="crossreference">hang out</span>.
