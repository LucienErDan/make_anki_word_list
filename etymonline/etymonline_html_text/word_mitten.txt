(from mid-13c. in surnames) &quot;a glove, a covering for the hand,&quot; especially &quot;a covering for the hand, differing from a glove in not having a separate covering for each finger, the thumb only being separated,&quot; from Old French <span class="foreign">mitaine</span> &quot;mitten, half-glove&quot; (12c.) and from Medieval Latin <span class="foreign">mitta</span>, both of uncertain
