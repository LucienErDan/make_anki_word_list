<p>Nautical sense of &quot;leave of absence&quot; is from 1758. Sense of &quot;privileges by grant&quot; (14c.) led to sense of &quot;a person&#x27;s private land&quot; (mid-15c.), within which certain special privileges may be exercised, which yielded in 18c. in both England and America a sense of &quot;a district within a county but having its own justice of the peace,&quot; and also &quot;a district adjacent to a city and in some degree under its municipal jurisdiction&quot; (as in <span class="foreign">Northern Liberties</span> of Philadelphia). Also compare Old French <span class="foreign">libertés</span> &quot;local rights, laws, taxes.&quot;</p>
