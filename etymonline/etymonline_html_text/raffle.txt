The word&#x27;s notions of &quot;opportunity&quot; and &quot;randomness&quot; are as old as the record of it in English and now all but crowd out its original notion of &quot;mere occurrence.&quot; Meaning &quot;fortuity, absence of any cause why an event should happen or turn out as it does, variability viewed as a real agent&quot; is from c. 1400.</p>
