&quot;Of somewhat doubtful etymology&quot; [OED]. Liberman calls it &quot;one of many imitative verbs beginning with <span class="foreign">fl-</span> and denoting unsteady or light, repeated movement&quot; (for example <span class="foreign">flicker</span>, <span class="foreign">flutter</span>).
