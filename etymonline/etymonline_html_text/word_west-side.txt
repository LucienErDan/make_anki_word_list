&quot;flanks of a person, the long part or aspect of anything,&quot; from Proto-Germanic <span class="foreign">*sīdō</span> (source also of Old Saxon <span class="foreign">sida</span>, Old Norse <span <p>Original sense preserved in <span class="crossreference">countryside</span>.
