<p>The <span class="foreign">-u-</span> is an Anglo-French insertion (see <span class="crossreference">gu-</span>); it was not originally pronounced. <blockquote>Mercii, þat beeþ men of myddel Engelond[,] vnderstondeþ bettre þe side langages, norþerne and souþerne, þan norþerne and souþerne vnderstondeþ eiþer oþer.
