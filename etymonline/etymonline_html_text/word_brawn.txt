<p>So called because the shape and movement of some muscles (notably biceps) were thought to resemble mice. <blockquote>Musclez &amp; lacertez bene one selfe þing, Bot þe muscle is said to þe fourme of mouse &amp; lacert to þe fourme of a lizard. [Guy de Chauliac, &quot;Grande Chirurgie,&quot; c. 1425]</blockquote>
