            <div class="row"><div class="thumbnail" id="yd-etym"><dl><dt><a target="_blank" href="/etym/s/compress">compress</a></dt><dd><b>compress: </b><span>see <a href="/etym/s/press" target="_blank">press</a></span><br></dd><dt><a target="_blank" href="/etym/s/compress">compress (v.)</a></dt><dd>late 14c., "to press (something) together," from Old French <span class="foreign">compresser</span> "compress, put under pressure," from Latin <span class="foreign">compressare</span> "to press together," frequentative of <span class="foreign">comprimere</span> "to squeeze," from <span class="foreign">com-</span> "together" (see <a href="/etym/s/com-" target="_blank">com-</a>) + <span class="foreign">premere</span> "to press" (see <a href="/etym/s/press" target="_blank">press</a> (v.1)).             <div class="row" id="yd-liju">
                 <dl><dt>1. He never understood how to organize or <b>compress</b> large masses of material. </dt> <dd>他从来就不懂如何对大篇的材料进行整理或精简。</dd> <p>来自柯林斯例句</p> </dl><dl><dt>2. Wood blocks may <b>compress</b> a great deal under pressure. </dt> <dd>木块受压时可缩小很多.</dd> <p>来自《现代英汉综合大词典》</p> </dl><dl><dt>4.
