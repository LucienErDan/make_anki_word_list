                        <div class="page-header"><h3>记忆方法</h3></div>
            <div class="row" style="font-family:SimSun,serif;">1、per- "thoroughly" + -ceiv- + -e.<br />
2、literally "take entirely".<br />
3、figuratively "grasp with the mind, learn, comprehend, recognize, notice".<br />
4、如今，在英语里只使用其比喻义、引申义。</div>

                                                <div class="page-header"><h3>中文词源</h3></div>
            <div class="row" id="yd-ciyuan" style="font-family:SimSun,serif;"><span class="ciyuan-title"><a target="_blank" href="/ciyuan/s/perceive">perceive</a> 感觉，感知</span><p>来自拉丁语percipere,得到，抓住，来自per-,完全的，-cip,抓住，理解，词源同conceive,percipient.引申词义感觉，感知。</p></div>
                                                <div class="page-header"><h3>英语词源</h3></div>
            <div class="row"><div class="thumbnail" id="yd-etym"><dl><dt><a target="_blank" href="/etym/s/perceive">perceive (v.)</a></dt><dd>c. 1300, via Anglo-French <span class="foreign">parceif</span>, Old North French <span class="foreign">*perceivre</span> (Old French <span class="foreign">perçoivre</span>) "perceive, notice, see; recognize, understand," from Latin <span class="foreign">percipere</span> "obtain, gather, seize entirely, take possession of," also, figuratively, "to grasp with the mind, learn, comprehend," literally "to take entirely," from <span class="foreign">per</span> "thoroughly" (see <a href="/etym/s/per" target="_blank">per</a>) + <span class="foreign">capere</span> "to grasp, take" (see <a href="/etym/s/capable" target="_blank">capable</a>).&#13; <br /><br />&#13; Replaced Old English <span class="foreign">ongietan</span>. Both the Latin senses were in Old French, though the primary sense of Modern French <span class="foreign">percevoir</span> is literal, "to receive, collect" (rents, taxes, etc.), while English uses the word almost always in the metaphorical sense. Related: <span class="foreign">Perceived</span>; <span class="foreign">perceiving</span>.</dd></dl></div></div>
                                    <div class="page-header"><h3><a href="/liju/w/perceive">权威例句</a></h3></div>
            <div class="row" id="yd-liju">
                 <dl><dt>1. Voters <b>perceive</b> him as a decisive and resolute international leader. </dt> <dd>投票者认为他是一位坚定果断的国际领导人。</dd> <p>来自柯林斯例句</p> </dl><dl><dt>2. "Precisely what other problems do you <b>perceive</b>?" she asked. </dt> <dd>“你究竟还发现了什么问题？”她问道。</dd> <p>来自柯林斯例句</p> </dl><dl><dt>3. They only recognise badness when they <b>perceive</b> it in others. </dt> <dd>他们只看到别人身上的恶行。</dd> <p>来自柯林斯例句</p> </dl><dl><dt>4. Did you <b>perceive</b> a red colour or a green one? </dt> <dd>你看出来是红颜色还是绿颜色?</dd> <p>来自《简明英汉词典》</p> </dl><dl><dt>5. I can't <b>perceive</b> any difference between these coins. </dt> <dd>我看不出这些硬币的区别.</dd> <p>来自《简明英汉词典》</p> </dl>                 <dl><p>[ <a href="/liju/w/perceive">perceive 造句</a> ]</p></dl>
