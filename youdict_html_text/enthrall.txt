                        <div class="page-header"><h3>记忆方法</h3></div>
            <div class="row" style="font-family:SimSun,serif;">1. slave => slav- => *thlav- => *thrav- => *thrau- => thrall.<br />
2. 这是一种缺乏理据性的根据音变规律和原理做出的一种联想和近义关联。其目的在于使单词更易于理解和记忆。<br />
3. 因此这种联想不是天马行空地乱联想，而是根据音变规律做出的一种比较合理的联想。<br />
4. en- "make, put in" + thrall.<br />
5. => hold in mental bondage.<br />
6. enthrall 的变体：enthral (en- + -thral [thrall]).<br />
7. Enthrall: en 因thrall（情）丝（缠）绕，陷入情网----迷惑，迷住。猪八戒进盘丝洞也是被蜘蛛精的丝绕了，所以被迷惑，迷住了。</div>

            <div class="row"><div class="thumbnail" id="yd-etym"><dl><dt><a target="_blank" href="/etym/s/enthrall">enthrall (v.)</a></dt><dd>also <span class="foreign">enthral</span> "to hold in mental or moral bondage," 1570s, from <a href="/etym/s/en-" target="_blank">en-</a> (1) "make, put in" + <a href="/etym/s/thrall" target="_blank">thrall</a> (n.).
