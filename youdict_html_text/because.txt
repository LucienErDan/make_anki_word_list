                                                <div class="page-header"><h3>中文词源</h3></div>
            <div class="row" id="yd-ciyuan" style="font-family:SimSun,serif;"><span class="ciyuan-title"><a target="_blank" href="/ciyuan/s/because">because</a> 因为</span><p>前缀be-, 强调。cause, 原因。</p></div>
                                                <div class="page-header"><h3>英语词源</h3></div>
            <div class="row"><div class="thumbnail" id="yd-etym"><dl><dt><a target="_blank" href="/etym/s/because">because</a></dt><dd><b>because: </b><span>[14] <i>Because </i>originated in the phrase <i>by cause</i>, which was directly modelled on French <i>par cause</i>. At first it was always followed by <i>of </i>or by a subordinate clause introduced by <i>that </i>or <i>why</i>: ‘The Holy Ghost was not yet given; because that Jesus was not yet glorified’, <i>St John’s Gospel</i>, 7:39, 1611. But already by the end of the 14th century <i>that </i>and <i>why </i>were beginning to be omitted, leaving <i>because </i>to function as a conjunction, a move which would perhaps have exercised contemporary linguistic purists as much as ‘The reason is because …’ does today. The abbreviated form <i>’cause </i>first appears in print in the 16th century.</span><br><span class="cognate">=&gt; <a href="/etym/s/cause" target="_blank">cause</a></span></dd><dt><a target="_blank" href="/etym/s/because">because (conj.)</a></dt><dd>c. 1300, <span class="foreign">bi cause</span> "by cause," modeled on French <span class="foreign">par cause</span>. Originally a phrase, often followed by a subordinate clause introduced by <span class="foreign">that</span> or <span class="foreign">why</span>. One word from c. 1400. As an adverb from late 14c. Clipped form <span class="foreign">cause</span> attested in writing by mid-15c.</dd></dl></div></div>
                                    <div class="page-header"><h3><a href="/liju/w/because">权威例句</a></h3></div>
            <div class="row" id="yd-liju">
                 <dl><dt>1. She feels devalued <b>because</b> she knows her husband has had affairs. </dt> <dd>她觉得自己掉价了，因为她知道丈夫有了外遇。</dd> <p>来自柯林斯例句</p> </dl><dl><dt>2. I denied my father <b>because</b> I wanted to become someone else. </dt> <dd>我和父亲断绝了关系，因为我想成为一个不一样的自己。</dd> <p>来自柯林斯例句</p> </dl><dl><dt>3. I get very nervous <b>because</b> I'm using a lot of expensive equipment. </dt> <dd>因为正在使用很多昂贵的设备，所以我非常紧张。</dd> <p>来自柯林斯例句</p> </dl><dl><dt>4. <b>Because</b> you're not burning calories, everything you eat turns to fat. </dt> <dd>由于没有消耗卡路里，你吃的所有东西都会变成脂肪。</dd> <p>来自柯林斯例句</p> </dl><dl><dt>5. He was not a particularly good shot <b>because</b> of his eyesight. </dt> <dd>他视力不好，算不上是一个出色的射手。</dd> <p>来自柯林斯例句</p> </dl>                 <dl><p>[ <a href="/liju/w/because">because 造句</a> ]</p></dl>
