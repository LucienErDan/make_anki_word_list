<p>Properly, the offspring of a he-ass and a mare; that of a she-ass and a stallion is technically a <span class="foreign">hinny.</span> The males are ordinarily incapable of procreation. <blockquote>I am a suthern man, I can not geste &#x27;rum, ram, ruf&#x27; by letter. [Chaucer, &quot;Parson&#x27;s Prologue and Tale&quot;]</blockquote>
<p>After the Norman-French invasion of 1066, the peoples of the island were distinguished as <span class="foreign">English</span> and <span class="foreign">French</span>, but after a few generations all were <span class="foreign">English</span>, and Latin-speaking scribes, who knew and cared little about Germanic history, began to use <span class="foreign">Anglo-Saxones</span> to refer to the pre-1066 inhabitants and their descendants. <blockquote>In the last years of the reign of Elizabeth, Camden revived the use of the old name <span class="foreign">Anglosaxones,</span> and, probably for the first time, used <span class="foreign">lingua Anglosaxonica</span> for the language of England before the Norman conquest.
