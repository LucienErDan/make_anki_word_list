title="Origin and meaning of costume" href="/word/costume#etymonline_v_19135"><em>costume</em> (n.)</a><section class="word__defination--2q7ZH undefined"><p>1715, &quot;style of dress,&quot; but also class="foreign">costume</span> (17c.), from Italian <span class="foreign">costume</span> &quot;fashion, habit,&quot; from Latin <span class="foreign">consuetudinem</span> (nominative <span
