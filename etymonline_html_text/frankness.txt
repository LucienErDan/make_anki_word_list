<blockquote><span class="foreign">Frank</span>, literally, <span class="foreign">free</span>; the freedom may be in regard to one&#x27;s own opinions, which is the same as <span class="foreign">openness</span>, or in regard to things belonging to others, where the freedom may go so far as to be unpleasant, or it may disregard conventional ideas as to reticence. <p>A generalization of the tribal name; the connection is that Franks, as the conquering class, alone had the status of freemen in a world that knew only free, captive, or slave.
