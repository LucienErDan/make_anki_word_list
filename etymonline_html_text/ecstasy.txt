<blockquote class="poetry">I am silvered sadness,</blockquote>
<blockquote class="poetry">I am moonlit gladness,</blockquote>
<blockquote class="poetry">I am that fine madness</blockquote>
<blockquote class="poetry">Of reverence half, and half of ecstasy</blockquote>
