<p>Replaced Old English <span class="foreign">andwlita</span> &quot;face, countenance&quot; (from root of <span class="foreign">wlitan</span> &quot;to see, look&quot;) and <span class="foreign">ansyn</span>, <span class="foreign">ansien</span>, the usual word (from the root of <span class="foreign">seon</span> &quot;see&quot;).
