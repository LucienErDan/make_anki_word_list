[&quot;The Heart of Islam: Enduring Values for Humanity,&quot; Seyyed Hossein Nasr, 2002]</blockquote>
<p><span class="foreign"><strong>Apple of Discord</strong></span> (c. 1400) was thrown into the wedding of Thetis and Peleus by Eris (goddess of chaos and discord), who had not been invited, and inscribed <span class="foreign">kallisti</span> &quot;To the Prettiest One.&quot; Paris, elected to choose which goddess should have it, gave it to Aphrodite, offending Hera and Athene, with consequences of the Trojan War, etc.</p>
<p><span class="foreign"><strong>Apple of one&#x27;s eye</strong></span> (Old English), symbol of what is most cherished, was the pupil, supposed to be a globular solid body. <span class="foreign"><strong>Apple-polisher</strong></span> &quot;one who curries favor&quot; first attested 1928 in student slang. The image in the phrase <span class="foreign"><strong>upset the apple cart</strong></span> &quot;spoil the undertaking&quot; is attested from 1788. <span
