<br/><br/>
The giant sequoia was unseen by Europeans until 1833 and unknown to scientists until 1852. In May 1855, a pair of American botanists named it <span class="foreign">Taxodium giganteum</span>, but that name was deemed inappropriate for several scientific reasons.
