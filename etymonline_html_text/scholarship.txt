<p>The alternative <span class="foreign">Indo-Germanic</span> (1835) was coined in German in 1823 (<span class="foreign">indogermanisch</span>), based on the two peoples then thought to be at the extremes of the geographic area covered by the languages, but this was before Celtic was realized also to be an Indo-European language.
