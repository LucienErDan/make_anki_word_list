<p>Meaning &quot;product of an untimely birth&quot; is from 1630s; earlier in this sense was <span class="foreign">abortive</span> (early 14c.). <p>In 19c. some effort was made to distinguish <span class="foreign">abortion</span> &quot;expulsion of the fetus between 6 weeks and 6 months&quot; from <span class="foreign">miscarriage</span> (the same within 6 weeks of conception) and <span class="foreign">premature labor</span> (delivery after 6 months but before due time). <blockquote><span class="foreign">Criminal abortion</span> is premeditated or intentional abortion procured, at any of pregnancy, by artificial means, and solely for the purpose of preventing the birth of a living child : feticide.
