Germanic. Perhaps originally the name of an individual tribe, but Gaulish (Celtic) origins have been proposed, from words perhaps originally meaning &quot;noisy&quot; (compare Old Irish <span class="foreign">garim</span> &quot;to shout&quot;) or &quot;neighbor&quot; (compare Old Irish <span class="foreign">gair</span> &quot;neighbor&quot;).
