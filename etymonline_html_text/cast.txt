In early use especially of dice, hence figurative uses relating to fortune or fate. Meaning &quot;that which is cast&quot; is from mid-15c. Meaning &quot;dash or shade of color&quot; is from c. 1600.<br/>
<br/>
The sense of &quot;a throw&quot; carried an idea of &quot;the form the thing takes after it has been thrown,&quot; which led to widespread and varied meanings, such as &quot;group of actors in a play&quot; (1630s). OED finds 42 distinct noun meaning and 83 verbal ones, with many sub-definitions. Many of the figurative senses converged in a general meaning &quot;sort, kind, style&quot; (mid-17c.). Meaning &quot;model made from taking an impression of an object&quot; is from c. 1500.
