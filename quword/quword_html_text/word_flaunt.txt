                                                <div class="page-header"><h3>中文词源</h3></div>
            <div class="row" id="yd-ciyuan" style="font-family:SimSun,serif;"><span class="ciyuan-title"><a target="_blank" href="/ciyuan/s/flaunt">flaunt</a> 炫耀</span><p>可能来自flout和vaunt的合成词。</p></div>
                                                <div class="page-header"><h3>英语词源</h3></div>
            <div class="row"><div class="thumbnail" id="yd-etym"><dl><dt><a target="_blank" href="/etym/s/flaunt">flaunt (v.)</a></dt><dd>1560s, "to display oneself in flashy clothes," of unknown origin. Perhaps a variant of <span class="foreign">flout</span> or <span class="foreign">vaunt</span>. Perhaps from Scandinavian, where the nearest form seems to be Swedish dialectal <span class="foreign">flankt</span> "loosely, flutteringly," from <span class="foreign">flakka</span> "to waver" (related to <a href="/etym/s/flag" target="_blank">flag</a> (v.1)). It looks French, but it corresponds to no known French word. Transitive sense, "flourish (something), show off, make an ostentatious or brazen display of" is from 1827. Related: <span class="foreign">Flaunted</span>; <span class="foreign">flaunting</span>; <span class="foreign">Flauntingly</span>.</dd><dt><a target="_blank" href="/etym/s/flaunt">flaunt (n.)</a></dt><dd>1620s, "act or habit of flaunting," from <a href="/etym/s/flaunt" target="_blank">flaunt</a> (v.).</dd></dl></div></div>
                                    <div class="page-header"><h3><a href="/liju/w/flaunt">权威例句</a></h3></div>
            <div class="row" id="yd-liju">
                 <dl><dt>1. One secret he learned very early on was not to <b>flaunt</b> his success. </dt> <dd>他很早就学到的一个秘诀就是不要夸耀自己的成功。</dd> <p>来自柯林斯例句</p> </dl><dl><dt>2. <b>Flaunt</b> your age and you must not have much longer to live. </dt> <dd>卖老要活不长的.</dd> <p>来自汉英文学 - 围城</p> </dl><dl><dt>3. He flaunts his queer style as a savage might <b>flaunt</b> a top - hat retrieved from somebody's dustbin. </dt> <dd>他对自己那种怪异的文体加以夸耀这恰似一个野人从别人的垃圾箱里捡出一顶礼帽时那种洋洋得意的样子.</dd> <p>来自辞典例句</p> </dl><dl><dt>4. Abstract: Luxury consumption is always connected with motivation of <b>flaunt</b>. </dt> <dd>摘要: 消费的奢侈性通常被认为与炫耀动机有关.</dd> <p>来自互联网</p> </dl><dl><dt>5. Why would you <b>flaunt</b> that on a public forum? </dt> <dd>为什么你们会在公共论坛大肆炫耀?</dd> <p>来自互联网</p> </dl>                 <dl><p>[ <a href="/liju/w/flaunt">flaunt 造句</a> ]</p></dl>

            return false;
