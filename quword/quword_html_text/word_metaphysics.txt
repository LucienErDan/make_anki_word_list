                    <div class="row"><h3 id="yd-word">metaphysics</h3></div>
                                        <div class="row" id="yd-word-pron">英 [metə'fɪzɪks]
美 [,mɛtə'fɪzɪks]</div>
                                        <div class="row" id="yd-word-meaning"><ul>
<li>n. 玄学，形而上学</li>
</ul></div>
                                            <div class="row" style="margin-bottom:6px;">

                                                <div class="page-header"><h3>中文词源</h3></div>
            <div class="row" id="yd-ciyuan" style="font-family:SimSun,serif;"><span class="ciyuan-title"><a target="_blank" href="/ciyuan/s/metaphysics">metaphysics</a> 形而上学，玄学</span><p>来自希腊语ta metaphysika,写在物理学后面的作品，来自meta,在后，physika,物理，词源同Physics.原指古希腊哲学家亚里士多德的编辑者在编辑完其物理方面的作品之后编辑其它作品所用的标题，后来被拉丁学者误解为超越物理的学问，即玄学，引申词义玄而上学。并由该词衍生出科学名词前缀meta-,元，始，玄。</p></div>
                                                <div class="page-header"><h3>英语词源</h3></div>
            <div class="row"><div class="thumbnail" id="yd-etym"><dl><dt><a target="_blank" href="/etym/s/metaphysics">metaphysics (n.)</a></dt><dd>1560s, plural of Middle English <span class="foreign">metaphisik</span>, <span class="foreign">methaphesik</span> (late 14c.), "branch of speculation which deals with the first causes of things," from Medieval Latin <span class="foreign">metaphysica</span>, neuter plural of Medieval Greek <span class="foreign">(ta) metaphysika</span>, from Greek <span class="foreign">ta meta ta physika</span> "the (works) after the Physics," title of the 13 treatises which traditionally were arranged after those on physics and natural sciences in Aristotle's writings. The name was given c.70 B.C.E. by Andronicus of Rhodes, and was a reference to the customary ordering of the books, but it was misinterpreted by Latin writers as meaning "the science of what is beyond the physical." See <a href="/etym/s/meta-" target="_blank">meta-</a> + <a href="/etym/s/physics" target="_blank">physics</a>. The word originally was used in English in the singular; plural form predominated after 17c., but singular made a comeback late 19c. in certain usages under German influence.</dd></dl></div></div>
                                    <div class="page-header"><h3><a href="/liju/w/metaphysics">权威例句</a></h3></div>
            <div class="row" id="yd-liju">
                 <dl><dt>1. Promote materialist dialectics and oppose <b>metaphysics</b> and scholasticism. </dt> <dd>要提倡唯物辩证法,反对形而上学和烦琐哲学.</dd> <p>来自《现代汉英综合大词典》</p> </dl><dl><dt>2. There is an alliance between logic and <b>metaphysics</b>. </dt> <dd>逻辑与形而上学之间有类似之处.</dd> <p>来自《现代英汉综合大词典》</p> </dl><dl><dt>3. Kant's work on the critique of experience became ultimately indistinguishable from ontology and <b>metaphysics</b>. </dt> <dd>康德关于经验的评论著作最终与本体论和形而上学混为一谈。</dd> <p>来自柯林斯例句</p> </dl><dl><dt>4. <b>Metaphysics</b> is concerned with the description of the fundamental aspects of reality. </dt> <dd>形而上学研究的是对现实的根本方面的描述.</dd> <p>来自哲学部分</p> </dl><dl><dt>5. He investigates theology, and <b>metaphysics</b> and all sorts of subjects. </dt> <dd>他研究神学, 还有形而上学,还有各种各样的学问.</dd> <p>来自辞典例句</p> </dl>                 <dl><p>[ <a href="/liju/w/metaphysics">metaphysics 造句</a> ]</p></dl>
