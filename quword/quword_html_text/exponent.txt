                        <div class="page-header"><h3>记忆方法</h3></div>
            <div class="row" style="font-family:SimSun,serif;">1. ex- + pon- + ent.<br />
2. 字面含义：put out, place out, put forth. => expound, one who expounds.<br />
3. 指数：ex- "upward" + pon- + -ent. 表明指数放在既在上面又在外面的位置上。</div>

                                                <div class="page-header"><h3>中文词源</h3></div>
            <div class="row" id="yd-ciyuan" style="font-family:SimSun,serif;"><span class="ciyuan-title"><a target="_blank" href="/ciyuan/s/exponent">exponent</a> 拥护者，鼓吹者，指数</span><p>ex-, 向外。-pon, 放置，词源同pose, component.即展开，描述美好的前景，引申义拥护，鼓吹。同时用来指数学术语指数（据说来自笛尔卡）。</p></div>
                                                <div class="page-header"><h3>英语词源</h3></div>
            <div class="row"><div class="thumbnail" id="yd-etym"><dl><dt><a target="_blank" href="/etym/s/exponent">exponent (n.)</a></dt><dd>1706, from Latin <span class="foreign">exponentem</span> (nominative <span class="foreign">exponens</span>), present participle of <span class="foreign">exponere</span> "put forth" (see <a href="/etym/s/expound" target="_blank">expound</a>). Earliest use is the mathematical one (said to have been introduced in algebra by Descartes) for the symbol to indicate by what power the base number is to be raised. The sense of "one who expounds" is 1812. As an adjective, from 1580s.</dd></dl></div></div>
