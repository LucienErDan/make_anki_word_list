                                                <div class="page-header"><h3>中文词源</h3></div>
            <div class="row" id="yd-ciyuan" style="font-family:SimSun,serif;"><span class="ciyuan-title"><a target="_blank" href="/ciyuan/s/impertinent">impertinent</a> 不相关的，不切题的，粗鲁的，无礼的</span><p>im-,不，非，pertinent,相关的。即不相关的，引申词义扯远的，过度的，粗鲁的，无礼的。</p></div>
                                                <div class="page-header"><h3>英语词源</h3></div>
            <div class="row"><div class="thumbnail" id="yd-etym"><dl><dt><a target="_blank" href="/etym/s/impertinent">impertinent (adj.)</a></dt><dd>late 14c., "unconnected, unrelated, not to the point," from Old French <span class="foreign">impertinent</span> (14c.) or directly from Late Latin <span class="foreign">impertinentem</span> (nominative <span class="foreign">impertinens</span>) "not belonging," literally "not to the point," from assimilated form of Latin <span class="foreign">in-</span> "not, opposite of" (see <a href="/etym/s/in-" target="_blank">in-</a> (1)) + <span class="foreign">pertinens</span> (see <a href="/etym/s/pertinent" target="_blank">pertinent</a>). Sense of "rudely bold" is 1680s, from earlier sense of "not appropriate to the situation," probably modeled on similar use in French, especially by Molière, from notion of meddling with what is beyond one's proper sphere.</dd></dl></div></div>
                                    <div class="page-header"><h3><a href="/liju/w/impertinent">权威例句</a></h3></div>
            <div class="row" id="yd-liju">
                 <dl><dt>1. Would it be <b>impertinent</b> to ask where exactly you were? </dt> <dd>能冒昧问一下您所在的确切位置吗？</dd> <p>来自柯林斯例句</p> </dl><dl><dt>2. I don't like strangers who ask <b>impertinent</b> questions. </dt> <dd>我不喜欢冒昧提问的陌生人。</dd> <p>来自柯林斯例句</p> </dl><dl><dt>3. Would it be <b>impertinent</b> to ask why you're leaving? </dt> <dd>问一下你为什么要离开不知是否唐突？</dd> <p>来自《权威词典》</p> </dl><dl><dt>4. After the <b>impertinent</b> way you acted, who could like you? </dt> <dd>由于你那鲁莽的做法, 谁还能喜欢你 呢 ?</dd> <p>来自《现代英汉综合大词典》</p> </dl><dl><dt>5. She is too free with her tongue and is rather <b>impertinent</b> to people. </dt> <dd>她讲话太随便,对人相当无礼.</dd> <p>来自《简明英汉词典》</p> </dl>                 <dl><p>[ <a href="/liju/w/impertinent">impertinent 造句</a> ]</p></dl>
