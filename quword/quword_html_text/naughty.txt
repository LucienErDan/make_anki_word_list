                                                <div class="page-header"><h3>中文词源</h3></div>
            <div class="row" id="yd-ciyuan" style="font-family:SimSun,serif;"><span class="ciyuan-title"><a target="_blank" href="/ciyuan/s/naughty">naughty</a> 顽皮的，淘气的</span><p>来自naught,零，没有。引申词义邪恶的，没有道德的，后主要用于指小孩顽皮淘气。</p></div>
                                                <div class="page-header"><h3>英语词源</h3></div>
            <div class="row"><div class="thumbnail" id="yd-etym"><dl><dt><a target="_blank" href="/etym/s/naughty">naughty (adj.)</a></dt><dd>late 14c., <span class="foreign">naugti</span> "needy, having nothing," from Old English <span class="foreign">nawiht</span> (see <a href="/etym/s/naught" target="_blank">naught</a>) + <a href="/etym/s/-y" target="_blank">-y</a> (2). Sense of "wicked, evil, morally wrong" is attested from 1520s; specific meaning "sexually promiscuous" is from 1869. The more tame main modern sense of "disobedient" (especially of children) is attested from 1630s. Related: <span class="foreign">Naughtily</span>; <span class="foreign">naughtiness</span>. A woman of bad character c. 1530-1750 might be called a <span class="foreign">naughty pack</span> (also sometimes of men and later of children).</dd></dl></div></div>
                                    <div class="page-header"><h3><a href="/liju/w/naughty">权威例句</a></h3></div>
            <div class="row" id="yd-liju">
                 <dl><dt>1. I've even seen her shake Zara when she's been <b>naughty</b>. </dt> <dd>我甚至见过她在扎拉淘气的时候使劲摇晃她。</dd> <p>来自柯林斯例句</p> </dl><dl><dt>2. You know what little boys are like with <b>naughty</b> words. </dt> <dd>你知道小男孩们说下流话是什么样子。</dd> <p>来自柯林斯例句</p> </dl><dl><dt>3. You <b>naughty</b> boy, you gave me such a fright. </dt> <dd>你这调皮鬼，吓了我一跳。</dd> <p>来自柯林斯例句</p> </dl><dl><dt>4. Girls, you're being very <b>naughty</b>. </dt> <dd>姑娘们，你们真是淘气。</dd> <p>来自柯林斯例句</p> </dl><dl><dt>5. I'm being very <b>naughty</b>—I've ordered champagne! </dt> <dd>我今天放肆一回—我要了香槟了！</dd> <p>来自《权威词典》</p> </dl>                 <dl><p>[ <a href="/liju/w/naughty">naughty 造句</a> ]</p></dl>
