                                                <div class="page-header"><h3>中文词源</h3></div>
            <div class="row" id="yd-ciyuan" style="font-family:SimSun,serif;"><span class="ciyuan-title"><a target="_blank" href="/ciyuan/s/rapport">rapport</a> 融洽，和谐</span><p>来自法语 rapporter,带回，来自 re-,往回，ap-,向，-port,带，承载，词源同 comport,portable. 引申词义恢复，融洽，和谐。</p></div>
                                                <div class="page-header"><h3>英语词源</h3></div>
            <div class="row"><div class="thumbnail" id="yd-etym"><dl><dt><a target="_blank" href="/etym/s/rapport">rapport (n.)</a></dt><dd>1660s, "reference, relation, relationship," from French <span class="foreign">rapport</span> "bearing, yield, produce; harmony, agreement, intercourse," back-formation from <span class="foreign">rapporter</span> "bring back; refer to," from <span class="foreign">re-</span> "again" (see <a href="/etym/s/re-" target="_blank">re-</a>) + <span class="foreign">apporter</span> "to bring," from Latin <span class="foreign">apportare</span> "to bring," from <span class="foreign">ad-</span> "to" (see <a href="/etym/s/ad-" target="_blank">ad-</a>) + <span class="foreign">portare</span> "to carry" (see <a href="/etym/s/port" target="_blank">port</a> (n.1)).&#13; <br /><br />&#13; Psychological meaning "intense harmonious accord," as between therapist and patient, is first attested 1894, though the word had been used in a very similar sense with reference to mesmerism from 1845 (first recorded in Poe). See also <a href="/etym/s/report" target="_blank">report</a> (n.). Johnson frowns on the word and credits its use in English to Sir William Temple, naturalizer of French terms, who did use it but was not the first to do so.</dd></dl></div></div>
                                    <div class="page-header"><h3><a href="/liju/w/rapport">权威例句</a></h3></div>
            <div class="row" id="yd-liju">
                 <dl><dt>1. He said he wanted "to establish a <b>rapport</b> with the Indian people" </dt> <dd>他说他想“同印度人民建立友好关系”。</dd> <p>来自柯林斯例句</p> </dl><dl><dt>2. She understood the importance of establishing a close <b>rapport</b> with clients. </dt> <dd>她懂得与客户建立密切和谐的关系的重要性。</dd> <p>来自《权威词典》</p> </dl><dl><dt>3. She has an excellent <b>rapport</b> with her staff. </dt> <dd>她跟她职员的关系非常融洽.</dd> <p>来自《简明英汉词典》</p> </dl><dl><dt>4. You have an intellectual <b>rapport</b>, a kind of easy companionship that makes me really jealous. </dt> <dd>你机智友善、平易近人，真是让我嫉妒。</dd> <p>来自柯林斯例句</p> </dl><dl><dt>5. If you have a good <b>rapport</b> with a dealer, they will always let you have first refusal on anything interesting. </dt> <dd>如果你和经销商关系好，好东西他们会任你先挑。</dd> <p>来自柯林斯例句</p> </dl>                 <dl><p>[ <a href="/liju/w/rapport">rapport 造句</a> ]</p></dl>
