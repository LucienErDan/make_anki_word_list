                                                <div class="page-header"><h3>中文词源</h3></div>
            <div class="row" id="yd-ciyuan" style="font-family:SimSun,serif;"><span class="ciyuan-title"><a target="_blank" href="/ciyuan/s/remember">remember</a> 记忆，想起，纪念</span><p>来自拉丁语 rememorori,记忆，想起，来自 re-,再，重新，memorari,记忆，词源同 memory. 拼写受 member 影响俗化。</p></div>
                                                <div class="page-header"><h3>英语词源</h3></div>
            <div class="row"><div class="thumbnail" id="yd-etym"><dl><dt><a target="_blank" href="/etym/s/remember">remember</a></dt><dd><b>remember: </b><span>[14] Latin <i>memor </i>meant ‘mindful’ (it gave English <i>memorial</i>, <i>memory</i>, etc, and went back ultimately to the Indo-European base *<i>men</i>-, *<i>mon</i>- ‘think’, source of a wide range of English vocabulary from <i>comment </i>to <i>mind</i>). From it in the post-classical period was formed the verb <i>rememorārī </i>‘recall to mind’, which passed into English via Old French <i>remembrer</i>.</span><br><span class="cognate">=&gt; <a href="/etym/s/comment" target="_blank">comment</a>, <a href="/etym/s/mental" target="_blank">mental</a>, <a href="/etym/s/mind" target="_blank">mind</a></span></dd><dt><a target="_blank" href="/etym/s/remember">remember (v.)</a></dt><dd>early 14c., "keep in mind, retain in the memory," from Old French <span class="foreign">remembrer</span> "remember, recall, bring to mind" (11c.), from Latin <span class="foreign">rememorari</span> "recall to mind, remember," from <span class="foreign">re-</span> "again" (see <a href="/etym/s/re-" target="_blank">re-</a>) + <span class="foreign">memorari</span> "be mindful of," from <span class="foreign">memor</span> "mindful" (see <a href="/etym/s/memory" target="_blank">memory</a>). Meaning "recall to mind" is late 14c.; sense of "to mention" is from 1550s. Also in Middle English "to remind" (someone). An Anglo-Saxon verb for it was <span class="foreign">gemunan</span>.</dd></dl></div></div>
                                    <div class="page-header"><h3><a href="/liju/w/remember">权威例句</a></h3></div>
            <div class="row" id="yd-liju">
                 <dl><dt>1. <b>Remember</b>, keep a positive attitude and good things will happen. </dt> <dd>记住：保持乐观的心态，好事自然会发生。</dd> <p>来自美剧《摩登家庭》</p> </dl><dl><dt>2. When life gets hard and you want to give up, <b>remember</b> that life is full of ups and downs, and without the downs, the ups would mean nothing. </dt> <dd>当生活很艰难，你想要放弃的时候，请记住，生活充满了起起落落，如果没有低谷，那站在高处也失去了意义。</dd> <p>来自</p> </dl><dl><dt>3. <b>Remember</b>, happiness doesn't depend upon who you are or what you have; it depends solely upon what you think.--Dale Carnegie </dt> <dd>请记住，幸福不在于你是谁或者你拥有什么，而仅仅取决于你的心态！</dd> <p>来自金山词霸 每日一句</p> </dl><dl><dt>4. I distinctly <b>remember</b> wishing I had not got involved. </dt> <dd>我清楚地记得希望自己没有被牵扯进去。</dd> <p>来自柯林斯例句</p> </dl><dl><dt>5. I try to <b>remember</b> all the good times I've had here. </dt> <dd>我试着回忆在这里度过的所有美好时光。</dd> <p>来自柯林斯例句</p> </dl>                 <dl><p>[ <a href="/liju/w/remember">remember 造句</a> ]</p></dl>

    document.getElementById('yd-search').onsubmit = function() {

        if (word == '') {
            return false;
        } else if (word.search(/^[\w \-]+$/) == 0) {
            top.location.href = '/w/' + encodeURIComponent(word.replace(/ /g, '_'));
            return false;

                var char_code = match.charCodeAt(0) - 0xfee0;
                return String.fromCharCode(char_code);
            });
        }
