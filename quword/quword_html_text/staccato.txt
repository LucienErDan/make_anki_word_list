                        <div class="page-header"><h3>记忆方法</h3></div>
            <div class="row" style="font-family:SimSun,serif;">1. detach is shortened form of destach-.<br />
2. destach- => distacc- (Italian variant form), and later shortened to stacc-.<br />
3. stacc- "detach" + -ato (Italian past participle suffix).<br />
4. => literally "detached, disconnected".<br />
5. staccato断奏,不连贯的声音。 s=dis-分开。 -tacc-=-tach-扣住,连接。 stacc-=detach使分开。 -ate的,-o音乐词尾。 -ato复合名词尾,表示音乐。 staccato即断开连接的音乐。</div>

                                                <div class="page-header"><h3>中文词源</h3></div>
            <div class="row" id="yd-ciyuan" style="font-family:SimSun,serif;"><span class="ciyuan-title"><a target="_blank" href="/ciyuan/s/staccato">staccato</a> 断音的，断奏的</span><p>来自意大利语 staccato,断开的，脱落的，过去分词格于 staccare,使分开，断开，缩写自 distaccare, 使分开，断开，来自 dis-,分开，-stacc,连接，钉上，词源同 attach,stack,stick.后用于音乐术语 指断音的，断奏的。</p></div>
                                                <div class="page-header"><h3>英语词源</h3></div>
            <div class="row"><div class="thumbnail" id="yd-etym"><dl><dt><a target="_blank" href="/etym/s/staccato">staccato (adj.)</a></dt><dd>1724, from Italian <span class="foreign">staccato</span>, literally "detached, disconnected," past participle of <span class="foreign">staccare</span> "to detach," shortened form of <span class="foreign">distaccare</span> "separate, detach," from Middle French <span class="foreign">destacher</span>, from Old French <span class="foreign">destachier</span> "to detach" (see <a href="/etym/s/detach" target="_blank">detach</a>). As an adverb from 1844. Related: <span class="foreign">Staccatissimo</span>.</dd></dl></div></div>

            <div class="row" id="yd-liju">

He spoke in Arabic, a short <b>staccato</b> burst. </dt> <dd>他突然用阿拉伯语叽里呱啦说了一串话。</dd> <p>来自柯林斯例句</p> </dl><dl><dt>2. a peculiar <b>staccato</b> voice </dt> <dd>特有的不连贯而且刺耳的嗓音</dd> <p>来自《权威词典》</p> </dl><dl><dt>3. Her pencil tapped out a <b>staccato</b> rhythm on the desk top. </dt> <dd>她用铅笔断断续续地敲击着桌面.</dd> <p>来自《简明英汉词典》</p> </dl><dl><dt>4. On the street , firecrackers were bursting like the <b>staccato</b> hoofbeats of stampeding horses. </dt> <dd>外面万马奔腾似的爆竹声送进他的耳里.</dd> <p>来自汉英文学 - 家（1-26） - 家（1-26）</p> </dl><dl><dt>5.
