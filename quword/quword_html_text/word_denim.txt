                        <div class="page-header"><h3>记忆方法</h3></div>
            <div class="row" style="font-family:SimSun,serif;">1. 又被音译为“丹宁布，丁尼布”，俗称牛仔布。是一种较粗厚的色织经面斜纹棉布，专门用来制作牛仔裤，经纱颜色深，一般为靛蓝色，纬纱颜色浅，一般为浅灰或煮练后的本白纱。又称靛蓝劳动布。始于美国西部，放牧人员用以制作衣裤而得名。<br />
2. 牛仔布本来只有蓝色，最初只是用来当帆布用，但现时已有多种不同的颜色，可用来配衬不同的衣饰。<br />
3. 牛仔布的英文名Denim源自法国的Nimes(尼姆市)，所以当地生产的布被称为de Nim，即是“来自Nimes的”。de 在法语中是“of”的意思，所以其意思是“of Nimes”.<br />
<br />
在五百多年前，意大利的航海家哥伦布发现了新大陆。当时航海的船只用的帆，是由一种非常坚韧而实用的粗糙布料做成。这种粗糙布料原产于法国一个小镇NIMES，所以就以法文取名 “Serge De Nimes”，而后被人们简称为DENIM。<br />
<br />
而在三百六十年后，一位在美国的犹太商人Levi Strausss把一种织料粗糙的帆布引入美国，因而缔造了牛仔裤历史性的一页，同时也建立了一个牛仔文化的神话王国。</div>

            <div class="row" id="yd-ciyuan" style="font-family:SimSun,serif;"><span class="ciyuan-title"><a target="_blank" href="/ciyuan/s/denim">denim</a> 牛仔布</span><p>来自法语serge de Nimes，因产自法国Nimes而得名。参照damask.</p></div>
                                                <div class="page-header"><h3>英语词源</h3></div>
            <div class="row"><div class="thumbnail" id="yd-etym"><dl><dt><a target="_blank" href="/etym/s/denim">denim</a></dt><dd><b>denim: </b><span>[17] The name of the fabric from which jeans are made had its origins in a sort of serge produced in the southern French town of Nîmes. The French naturally enough called it <i>serge de Nîmes</i>, but the original meaning of this soon became lost when English borrowed it as <i>serge de Nim</i>, and the last two words came to be run together as <i>denim</i>.</span><br></dd><dt><a target="_blank" href="/etym/s/denim">denim (n.)</a></dt><dd>1690s, from French <span class="foreign">serge de Nîmes</span> "serge from Nîmes," town in southern France. Application to "coarse cotton cloth" is first recorded 1850 in American English. <span class="foreign">Denims</span> "pants made of denim" recorded from 1868. The place name is Roman <span class="foreign">Nemausus</span>, said to be ultimately from Gaulish <span class="foreign">nemo</span> "sanctuary."</dd></dl></div></div>
