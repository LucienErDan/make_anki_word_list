                        <div class="page-header"><h3>记忆方法</h3></div>
            <div class="row" style="font-family:SimSun,serif;">1、Latin surdus "deaf, unheard, silent, mute". => surd-: deaf, unheard, silent, mute.<br />
2、surd- => surd: irrational.<br />
3、咱们汉语里有表达这种关联的形象的成语：掩耳盗铃、装聋作哑、充耳不闻；这些行为是不是都非常的荒唐、荒谬、无理取闹啊！因此，由词根含义转变到、引申到单词含义自然就水到渠成了。<br />
4、ab- "away, off; 由于该前缀的这层含义，在这里添加在surd之前更加强了surd的含义，为什么呢？想一想充耳不闻、装聋作哑地走开(away, off)，就明白了！" + surd- / surd + -ity => absurdity.<br />
5、In surd, the mathematical sense is from the use of Latin surdus to translate Arabic (jadhr) asamm "deaf (root)," itself a loan-translation of Greek alogos, literally "speechless, without reason".<br />
6、In French, sourd remains the principal word for "deaf".</div>

                                                                        <div class="page-header"><h3>英语词源</h3></div>
            <div class="row"><div class="thumbnail" id="yd-etym"><dl><dt><a target="_blank" href="/etym/s/absurdity">absurdity (n.)</a></dt><dd>late 15c., from Middle French <span class="foreign">absurdité</span>, from Late Latin <span class="foreign">absurditatem</span> (nominative <span class="foreign">absurditas</span>) "dissonance, incongruity," noun of state from Latin <span class="foreign">absurdus</span> "out of tune;" figuratively "incongruous, silly, senseless," from <span class="foreign">ab-</span>, intensive prefix, + <span class="foreign">surdus</span> "dull, deaf, mute" (see <a href="/etym/s/susurration" target="_blank">susurration</a>).</dd></dl></div></div>

            <div class="row" id="yd-liju">
